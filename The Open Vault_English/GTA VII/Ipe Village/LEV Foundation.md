**Affiliation:** Founded by Dr. Aubrey de Grey **Summary:** A biomedical research and advocacy charity dedicated to developing and demonstrating therapies aimed at repairing the damage of aging. It focuses on achieving "Longevity Escape Velocity" by addressing the distinct classes of damage that accumulate in the body, relevant to health, technology, and techno-optimism. **Relevant Work/Interests:** * Focuses on rejuvenation through the repair of molecular and cellular aging damage. * Funds research projects, including cryopreservation technology for organ replacement ("Transplants on Demand"). * Conducts large-scale mouse lifespan studies to test therapeutic combinations ("Robust Mouse Rejuvenation" program). * Aims to empirically demonstrate the feasibility and potential of comprehensive rejuvenation medicine. **Links:** * Website: https://www.levf.org/ * Transplants on Demand Project: https://www.levf.org/projects/transplants-on-demand * Robust Mouse Rejuvenation Study 1: https://www.levf.org/projects/robust-mouse-rejuvenation-study-1 * Robust Mouse Rejuvenation Study 2: https://www.levf.org/projects/robust-mouse-rejuvenation-study-2
