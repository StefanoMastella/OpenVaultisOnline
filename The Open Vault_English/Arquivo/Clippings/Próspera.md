**Affiliation:** Special Economic Zone in Roatán, Honduras.  
**Summary:** An innovative community and jurisdiction focused on revolutionizing economic development through flexible governance and economic policies. It seeks to unleash human potential and drive entrepreneurial success, serving as a laboratory for new forms of governance, finance, health (innovative treatments), and social organization, highly relevant to the themes and spirit of Ipê City.  
**Relevant Work/Interests:** Operates with a unique regulatory system and pro-business environment. Hosts the Bitcoin Center (relevant to Finance/Blockchain) and the Infinita City Dome (used for events and pitches, connecting to Ipê's pop-up city model). Fosters innovation in health (advanced therapies, longevity). Has a community of over 1700 residents and 200 businesses.
